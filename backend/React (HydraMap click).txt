React (HydraMap click)
  ↓
Zustand store (updates selected region)
  ↓
SelectedLocation.tsx (triggers API fetch)
  ↓
Fetch call: POST /climate/region/statistics
  ↓
FastAPI climate router
  ↓
ClimateService (application layer)
  ↓
ClimateRepository (domain port)
  ↓
S3ClimateRepository (infrastructure adapter):
  1. Loads climate raster from S3 (lazy)
  2. Retrieves region geometry from cached GeoDataFrame
  3. Reprojects geometry to raster CRS
  4. Masks raster with rioxarray.rio.clip()
  5. Computes mean/min/max
  ↓
RegionStatisticsResponse
  ↓
SelectedLocation.tsx (renders choropleth + stats)
